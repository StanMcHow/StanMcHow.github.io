Our brand information

Laura Ferrini is a modern brand, fresh breath in the design of wedding dresses.

The bride of Laura Ferrini is a young modern girl who is not afraid to experiment, to combine classical traditions and modern trends, which wants to look unsurpassed and sophisticated, who seeks to emphasize their individuality.

We understand that a wedding is a special event, which is the result of love, trust, days of stay together. This is a decisive step, which can only be in life once. Therefore, our team of designers is working inspiringly every day to make your wedding day unique and memorable so that you feel like a queen on this important occasion.
